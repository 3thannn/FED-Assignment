Running

Most Popular Way To Exercise

Running is the easiest form of Exercise one can get into. There is no need for a court, no need for any setup or equipment. Simply put on your running shoes and head out. With major sports brands like nike and addidas spending millions of research in developing sports/activewear and especially shoes, running has never been more popular than it is now.

Health Benefits

  • Better Mental Health

  • Reduced Stress

  • Improve Heart Health

  • Better Mood

  • Keeps BMI in check

Why is it effective?

There is a rule where running burns 100 calories per Mile, or 1.6 KM. Making running one of the best workouts for losing weight.





Locations

The Southern Ridges Trail

Cutting through 5 parks and nature reserves, with 10km of green surroundings, this trail is our top pick for running in SG. With slopes and curves, this trail will test your endurance and stamina.

McRitchie Reservoir

With the whole park covering 12 hectares of land filled with greenery and the reservoir itself, McRitchie is a great place for a jog, to breathe in fresh air and get away from the bustling life of the city.

Sports Hub Running Track

With the track spanning the whole circumfrence of the stadium, one round around the track is just shy of a kilometre at 888 metres!. It is also the largest, completely sheltered running track in Singapore. It is open 24/7 and open for the public to use for free.

Tips and Tricks

[Hover above the blank space below]

TIP 1

Invest in a pair of good shoes! Shoes are the only thing that seperates your feet and the ground when you run. Its simple. Comfortable shoes = Comfortable run

TIP 2

Break up your run into short burts if you are just starting off. Take short breaks in between. Remember its always good to keep your heart rate high, do not take too long of a break

TIP 3

Remember to hydrate yourself! During breaks, drink a mouth of water, dont overdrink though, you'll get a sharp pain in your tummy.

TIP 4

Take up running progressively. Start with short jogs, take runs near your neighbourhood, and make sure you know the route well before embarking on your run. Slowly, up the distance and pace when you get aqquited to it.
