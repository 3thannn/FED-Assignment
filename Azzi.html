Team Singapore


Team Singapore is a shared identity for all Singaporeans, and is one big family inspiring one another through sport. It is a relationship that extends beyond the sporting fraternity, and beyond the major Games.

Team Singapore is about living better lives as we embody values inherent in sport: a winning spirit, an appreciation for teamwork, a commitment to purpose, the importance of ethical behaviour and a love for our country.
